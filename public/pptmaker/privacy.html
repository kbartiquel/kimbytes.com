Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At KimBytes, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how PPTMaker ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Key Point: We collect minimal data and never sell your personal information to third parties.

Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose Storage
Presentation Topics To generate AI content Not stored
Generated Presentations Local access to your files Device only
Usage Statistics Track free tier limits Device only
Subscription Status Manage premium access RevenueCat

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service: Process your requests and generate presentations
  • Improve the App: Understand usage patterns to enhance features
  • Manage Subscriptions: Process payments and maintain premium access
  • Enforce Limits: Track usage for free tier restrictions

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Presentation content is processed but not permanently stored on our servers
  • Generated files are stored locally on your device
  • We do not have access to your saved presentations

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI / Anthropic: AI content generation (your prompts are processed according to their privacy policies)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and payment processing
  • Apple App Store: App distribution and in-app purchases

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data (presentations are stored locally and can be deleted from your device)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from any marketing communications
  • Portability: Export your presentations at any time

Children's Privacy

PPTMaker is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
